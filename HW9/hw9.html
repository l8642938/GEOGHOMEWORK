Map of Chapel Hill with surrounding streams, protected watershed area, and development areas.

Are new development sites located near watershed protected area? How close to streams or rivers are new developing sites?

To help answer this question I did a map of Chapel Hill with all of its streams, developing areas, and the Jordan Lake watershed protected area together. I clipped each of these so that it was only in the specific area of Chapel Hill that I wanted to test. It turns out that the developing sites are scattered throughout, but are all reletively close to streams or a source of water. I found this out by putting a buffer on the developing sites to learn how far they were from streams. In regards to the Jordan Lake protected watershed area I created a new buffer of these developing sites so one could see that many sites are being developed in these areas. This came as a shock to me as many ecolgical and environmental issues could arise as a result of this.
